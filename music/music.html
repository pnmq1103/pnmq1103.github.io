Favourite songs/albums/artists.

Requirements:

  1. Format:
    • Song: [Artist] - [Song] ([Year]).
    • Album: [Artist] - [Album] ([Year]).
    • Artist: [Artist].
    • If there is more than 1 artist then concatenate using `&&`.
  2. Must include images (i.e. cover art).
  3. Non-Latin scripts must include their standardized romanization.

SP/EP/LP?

  • SP: standard play, 1-3 songs under 15m.
  • EP: extended play, 4-7 songs under 30m.
  • LP: long play (album), 8-12+ songs up to 60-70m.
