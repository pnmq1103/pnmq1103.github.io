Lạc: Death Grips - Exmilitary (2011) Rating: 9.5/10
Youtube

The musical equivalent of brewing coffee with Everclear. The Hybrid Theory of alternative hip-hop. Suitable for cramming finals, going to war, and anything in between.

Notable tracks: Takyon (Death Yon)

Not the hardest song in the album, but probably the most catchy, the hard-hitting 'Takyon' serves as a good introduction to Death Grips' gritty and unique sound.

Quân: ひとひら(hitohira) - 円(en) (2025) Rating: 9/10
Spotify

The album is a journey. Notice that the first song is "Departure", the last song is "En" (circle) which represents the wholeness, cycleness. This one makes the album complete (pun intended). The transition between the songs is seamless. The lyrics is short but more than satisfactory. This is math rock, emo, and shoegaze.

Notable tracks: 十二月-Departure, 塔は白い(to wa shiroi), ひのめ(hinome), フェリス (ferris), 円(en)

These are very good. The rest is also very good. "Ferris" feels emotional (emo).

An: Kendrick Lamar - Mr.Morale & The Big Steppers (2022) Rating: 9.67/10

Kendrick keeps it real with this one. The tracks are both introspective and socially conscious. It's like he's giving advice to us through his lyricism and storytelling. Peak.

"If I didn't learn to love myself, forgive myself 400 times" hits hard.
