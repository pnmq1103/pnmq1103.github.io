Artists/Bands

Lạc: Rating: 9.75/10
Youtube channel

I don't listen to a lot of vocaloid, but Azari is one artist that I keep coming back to; everytime they release a song, I feel the need to listen to their entire discography again (which as of now consists of only 15 songs btw). Their sound design and instrumentals are incredible, resulting in every single track being equally unique and addictive. No one in the vocaloid scene uses drum and bass like Azari does; it is creative, deep, and hard-hitting - changing from song to song, and honestly reminds me more of western EDM and Bass House than vocaloid--their latest song, Scapegoat, even has a hardbass-sounding instrumental! I also like their recent collaboration with singer Rosu, whose voice is mellifluous yet intense enough to match the instrumental. Last but not least they release all their music, basically for free, with lyrics and all that, in a Google Drive folder, which is highkey cool af. Their first songs' titles on Youtube use non-Unicode characters which make them insanely hard to find if you don't know the name, which again is purely aura-farming and I just so fucking love it.

Notable tracks: (honestly you could, and I suggest you should, listen to all Azari's songs in one sitting)

  • Shadow Shadow

    Perhaps their most famous song. Catchy beat, interesting lyrics, deep bass, overall a nice introduction for the artist's style.

  • Wink (feat. Rosu)

    This song marks the start of the collaboration with singer Rosu. Her voice and intonation perfectly portrays the lyrics, which I intepret as being about a heartbroken yandere with mental issues.

  • Witch Hunt

    Sweet Lies

    These are experimental tracks imo as Azari strays away from their usual high-octance style for something darker (first track)/more melancholic (second one).

Phát: Hiperson - 成長小說[Thành Trường Tiểu Thuyết] (2020) Rating: 8.5/10
Youtube

God I love twin tails!! Uohhh! But to be honest, for some reason I believe that they are one of the greatest symbols for strength or even humanity. For the duality of twin tails, there is also the duality of shoegaze, they fall into either of these 2 categories: reminiscent and hope, or criticism and revolts. Nevertheless both are the 2 channels through which desires, wants, and connections reach the audience the deepest. In [成長小說], social criticism goes hands in hands with the realization and radicalization against that moment during the changing of times. When old ideas get replaced but before any new ideals take hold, there is that empty periods where there is nothing. Nothing to react to, nothing to rub against another, there is only the individual and their reflections. Growing up is not that different, I guess. The music is the environment that allow all of this to take place, which is in itself, immaculately well written.

Notable tracks: 春风[Xuân Phong], 我们的歌谣[Ngã Môn Đích Ca Dao], 找到了[Trảo Đáo Liễu]
